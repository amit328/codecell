Codecell Tinkerer's

Codecell is a technical department of Vivekanand Education Society's Institute of Technology, Chembur with an aim to empower the student community within our institution and the local ecosystem, and help them take their skills, knowledge and ideas to the next level and expand their skill set. We organize various workshops & seminars and provide students with various opportunities in Hackathons and Ideathons.

Themes Released!

THE GAMES OF YORE

Recreate one of history’s oldest games from around the world. The challenge is to create a user experience which is both informative and engaging as it guides them through the lost games from ancient times.

LOCKDOWN LAUGHTER

Bring out your inner prankster and hack your way into making people laugh or even cry , its upto your imagination :)

GO CONTACTLESS!

Your project should give contactless alternatives to some of the work that gets done in public sector/government institutions or any public agency which would otherwise have required direct human interaction.

The travel and tourism sector took a plunge for worse this year due to travel bans and the fear of covid 19. Pave the way to boost the tourism sector post the pandemic.

VIRTUAL GATHERING

Commitment

At CodeCell VESIT, we create an environment that brings passionate programmers together irrespective of their gender, race, sexual orientation, socioeconomic background or ethnicity.

Ever since its inception, over the years, we have been working hard to inculcate a healthy and competitive coding enviornment in VESIT by learning about new technologies in workshops and seminars and putting it to use in coding competitions and hackathons.

We work towards nuturing interest and developing coding skills and aim to help students learn about essential skills necessary for real world applications. We are commited to help students foster coding skills , spirit of learning, and prepare for prestigious coding contests and hackathons.
